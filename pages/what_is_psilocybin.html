According to Janikian (2019) psilocybin is a natural psychedelic alkaloid, which when consumed is broken down into psilocin and causes the psychedelic effects. In addition to this, Jackson (2015), mentions that psilocybin is a psychedelic compound which closely resembles serotonin and on its own is quite safe to consume. Nevertheless, he highlights that the user's set and setting play a large role in the outcome of the overall experience.

Terence McKenna quoted in Jackson (2015) mentions the following:

"Psychedelics are illegal not because a loving government is concerned that you may jump out of a third story window. Psychedelics are ilegal because they dissolve opinion structures and culturally laid down models of behavior and information processing. They open you up to the possibility that everything you know is wrong."
